Hello, I'm Mashego Sililo,
I'm a front end developer and blogger.

I work primarily with HTML, Javascript and React and work with styling languages and frameworks like CSS, SCSS and Bootstrap. I also have experience with Python and PHP. My work revolves around building fun and exciting user experiences for communities and having those communities be able to interact with the project and each other. On my blog that's so fetch(), I provide reviews, tips and tricks and resources to developers about API's.

I am interested in the type of tech tools that people use to solve problems and I have been able to dip my toe into that interest by getting involved in Open Source projects, notably uiGradients.

I love whales, ice-cream, fashion and art galleries. You'll most likely find me reading in a park or working through a sudoku puzzle.
